About Portfolio Partners

Cricket Ventures

Cricket Ventures is a forward-looking venture capital fund deeply rooted in the transformative potential of Web3 technologies. Since our inception in 2022, we have been at the forefront of investing in blockchain, decentralized finance (DeFi), non-fungible tokens (NFTs), and other cutting-edge innovations within the Web3 ecosystem. Our mission is to identify and support visionary founders and projects that are reshaping the future of finance, ownership, and digital interaction.

What sets Cricket Ventures apart is our commitment to being a high-touch fund. We believe in building strong, collaborative relationships with the teams we back, offering strategic guidance, mentorship, and access to our extensive network of web3 veterans. Our team consists of crypto natives who bring a wealth of experience from advising, investing in, and even founding their own successful projects and protocols. This hands-on approach allows us to not only provide capital but also to actively support our portfolio companies in navigating the complexities and opportunities of the rapidly evolving Web3 landscape.

At Cricket Ventures, we are passionate about driving meaningful innovation and empowering creators and entrepreneurs to unlock the full potential of web3.
